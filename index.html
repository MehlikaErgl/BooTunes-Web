Project Overview

BooTunes revolutionizes reading by combining AI-powered book analysis with personalized music recommendations to enhance your literary journey.

Chapter Summarization

Using Pegasus transformer model to generate concise, accurate summaries for each chapter.

Emotion Analysis

Advanced NLP techniques to detect emotional tone and sentiment in each chapter.

Music Recommendation

Curated playlists matching the emotional tone and themes of each chapter.

Visual Generation

Stable Diffusion creates atmospheric images based on chapter content.

How It Works

1

Upload or Select a Book

Users can upload their own books or choose from our curated library.

2
